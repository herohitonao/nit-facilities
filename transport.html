The National Institute of Technology (NIT) Manipur provides dedicated transport services to ensure safe, reliable, and convenient mobility for students, faculty, and staff. The Institute maintains a fleet of vehicles catering to different requirements, ranging from student conveyance to emergency services. The details are as follows:


  1. Bus - A bus service is provided exclusively for female students, facilitating daily pick-up and drop-off between Takyel Girls Hostel and the main campus. The service operates from 8:30 am to 5:30 pm, ensuring smooth and secure transit.
  2. Traveller - Similar to the bus facility, a Traveller van is arranged for the transportation of female students between Takyel Girls Hostel and the main campus, functioning from 8:30 am to 5:30 pm. This provides additional convenience and flexibility.
  3. Eco-van - For official purposes, an Eco-van is available from 9:00 am to 5:00 pm. This service supports administrative and academic mobility requirements.
  4. Ambulance Van - To address any health-related contingencies, the Institute operates a dedicated Ambulance Van on a 24x7 basis. This ensures immediate response and transportation during medical emergencies.
  5. Hired Bus - To support students residing in private hostels outside the campus, a hired bus service is arranged. The service runs between the hostels and the main campus from 8:30 am to 5:30 pm, thereby ensuring timely and safe conveyance for male students.

Through these transport arrangements, NIT Manipur demonstrates its commitment to student welfare, safety, and accessibility. The services not only address academic needs but also provide a reliable support system for emergencies and official work.
