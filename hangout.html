  1. Savings bank account and Current bank account facilities
  2. Retail loan facilities like Auto loan, Personal Loan, Housing Loan etc.
  3. Business Loan facilties
  4. Government sponsored facilities
  5. Internet Payment Gateway for NIT Manipur
  6. Merchant QR Code facility
  7. Salary account facility for the employee with inbuilt insurance schemes
  8. Various investment facilities like Mutual Funds, Life Insurance products, NFOs, Fixed Deposit, Recurring Deposit, PPF, Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana etc.
